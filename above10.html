Above €10

Do you want some inspiration for your bucketlist or just for something to do with someone? Here are some of the ideas! These are all above 10 euro's up to 100 euro's, it is what you make of it!

Holiday

Do you want to go on a holiday? But you don't know where? Take a suprise holiday. You sign yourself in and choose a date. A week before going away you get to know what the weather will be and a day before you get the location! How fun is that!

Visit museums

Museums are a really nice place to go to. Nowadays you have digital museums, with lights and 4d works. These kind of museums you can most of the time find in Amsterdam! One of them is Nyx and you have a lot more of them. If you don't like that, we also have normal museums with art you can see in real life. If you are a student you can take a look for a discount!

Shop it till you drop it, biggest mall in the netherlands!

Ofcourse, shopping belongs to the list! Go to the biggest mall in the netherlands and go to all the stores you want to! There are a lot of american stores that most of the other malls don't have. So cool right?!

Horseriding

If you want a more active adventure, try horseriding! You can hire a ranch to go with you to a beach! Or you can go on a vacation and go horseriding there! It's really fun, you should really try it.

Karaoke night!

A nice way of going out, karaoke night! How fun would it be to go singing with your friends. It would be in the middle of pricing. Entering around 30 euro's and then drinks and food is up to you!
